Managing rental properties in Ghana has long been a fragmented, manual process — spreadsheets for tracking rent, WhatsApp threads for maintenance issues, physical files for lease documents. Rentloop was built to change that.

Rentloop is an all-in-one property management platform designed specifically for the Ghana rental market. It gives property owners, managers, and landlords a single place to manage everything — from the moment a tenant applies to the day they move out.

## Who is Rentloop for?

Rentloop works for anyone who manages rental properties in Ghana:

- **Individual landlords** who own and rent out apartments, houses, or rooms
- **Property management companies** that manage properties on behalf of owners
- **Real estate agencies** handling multiple clients and portfolios
- **Developers** managing newly built units

Whether you have 2 units or 200, Rentloop scales with you. The first 5 units are completely free — no credit card required.

## Managing Properties and Units

Once you're set up, you can create as many properties as you need. Each property can have multiple **blocks** (buildings or sections) and each block can contain multiple **units**.

Rentloop supports all the unit types common in the Ghana market:

- **Apartment** — standard flats within a building
- **House** — standalone residential properties
- **Studio** — self-contained single-room units
- **Office** — commercial office spaces
- **Retail** — shops, stalls, and commercial units

Each unit has its own status — **Available**, **Occupied**, **Maintenance**, or **Draft** — so you always know what's ready to rent and what isn't.

## Two Ways to Onboard Tenants

When a tenant is ready to move in, Rentloop gives you two onboarding paths:

**Self-service onboarding** generates a unique application link you send directly to the tenant. They fill in their own details, upload identity documents, and submit — no back-and-forth needed.

**Admin-guided onboarding** lets you fill in everything on the tenant's behalf through a 5-step wizard. This covers personal information, identity verification, emergency contacts, employment or student status, and a final review before submission.

Both paths collect everything you need: full name, date of birth, Ghana Card or passport details, proof of income or admission, and emergency contacts.

## Lease Management

Once a tenant application is approved, Rentloop walks you through the full lease setup:

- Set the unit, lease term, and rental amount
- Generate and send lease documents for digital signing
- Track the signing status in real time
- Record the move-in date and confirm occupancy

All lease documents are stored securely and accessible at any time from the tenant's profile.

## Rent Collection

Rentloop supports flexible rent collection to match how landlords and tenants in Ghana actually operate:

**Payment frequencies** — Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Biannual, or Annual. You set the schedule that works for your property.

**Multi-currency** — collect rent in GHS (Ghana Cedis), USD, or EUR depending on the agreement.

**Payment methods** — tenants can pay via Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfer, or card. Every payment is logged and linked to the lease.

You get a clear record of who has paid, who hasn't, and what's outstanding — no more chasing payments manually.

## Maintenance Requests

Tenants can log maintenance requests directly through the Rentloop tenant app. Each request includes:

- A title and description of the issue
- **Priority level** — Low, Medium, High, or Emergency
- **Category** — Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, or General
- Photos or attachments of the problem

As a property manager, you can track every request, assign it internally, communicate through comments, and log repair expenses — all in one place. Tenants see updates in real time, so there's no ambiguity about what's happening with their request.

## Analytics and Reporting

Rentloop's dashboard gives you a live view of your portfolio's performance:

- Occupancy rates across all properties
- Rent collected vs outstanding
- Maintenance request trends
- Unit-level financial summaries

Whether you're managing 5 units or 150, you have the numbers you need to make good decisions.

## Team Collaboration

If you work with a team — property officers, accountants, or assistants — Rentloop lets you invite members with role-based permissions. Managers control exactly what each team member can view and do, so your data stays secure while your team stays productive.

## Mobile App for Tenants

Rentloop isn't just a tool for property managers. Tenants get their own dedicated mobile app (available on iOS and Android) where they can:

- View their lease and payment history
- Submit and track maintenance requests
- Receive announcements from property management
- Access important documents

This transparency reduces calls and messages to property managers and gives tenants confidence that they're being looked after.
